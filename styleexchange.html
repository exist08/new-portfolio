StyleExchange

A clothing rental app designed for events and festivals like Garba, weddings, or parties. Users can rent suits, kurtas, or dresses with filters for size, brand, and price. It also allows selling or renting out used clothes that no longer fit or are out of style.

Overview

StyleExchange makes fashion more accessible by enabling short-term rentals. Whether attending a cultural event or a formal gathering, users can borrow stylish clothes without committing to a purchase. The platform also promotes sustainability by encouraging people to rent or sell clothes that they no longer use.

Key Features

  • Filter rentals by size, brand, and price
  • Borrow clothes for festivals, weddings, and events
  • Option to sell or rent your own used clothes
  • Modern, mobile-first design for easy browsing

Tech Stack

React Native Node.js MongoDB TailwindCSS
